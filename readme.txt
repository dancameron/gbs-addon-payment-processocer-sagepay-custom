GBS Cart Disabler and Purchase Limit Enabler
============================================

Disables the ability to purchase multiple deals by limiting the GBS cart. Enables purchase limits on gateways that intentionally remove the functionality and modifies the payment gateways to run authorization and automatic capturing (limited).

Installation
------------

1. Upload and activate plugin.
2. Go to Group Buying > General Options, scroll to bottom of page and check the box for the "Cart Disabler and Purchase Limit Enabler" module you want to activate.
3. Go to Group Buying > General Settings, select the new payment gateway and save. Configure the newly selected payment module.
4. Test.
